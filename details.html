Kumasi Technical University: All you need to Know!

School

Formerly known as Kumasi Polytechnic, the Kumasi Technical University is one of the leading Technical Universities in Ghana if not the best. Its name and administration were changed in September 2016 when the government sought to upgrade a number of Polytechnics to technical university status.

Elsewhere polytechnics are very important. Not in Ghana. Many people don’t respect polytechnic education. Na who cause am! Apparently, just the name change is a step in the right direction. Let’s look into the dynamics and prospects of the new Kumasi Technical University. Let’s dive in.

What is Kumasi Technical University?

Crest
Kumasi Technical University has a single aim and obligation – to produce graduates who are relevant to the industry. How would they do this?

Linking in-school training with on-the-job training provides life-transforming opportunities and experiences for students to gain and apply learned knowledge and skills at the workplace. That is their topmost proposition to attract students to the institution. And are they succeeding with this objective?

Let’s buckle up and dive in.
